Target audience

The target audience of the weatheraway.com is: Local families, tourists, tornato enthusiasts and others interested in the weather of the town weatherahead is based in.
Age: 20+
Motivation: To Learn more about the weather and attractions of the town weatherahead is based in.

Personas

First persona: A woman in her forties. She is a local mother interestested in gardening, football, and jogging. She has three children, two girls and a boy. Her children are all in after-school activities, mainly sports.
Occupation: Stay at home mom
Education: Has a bachelor's degree in education. Goals and motivation for using the site: To keep track of the weather, and to keep up with her town's current events.
Second persona: A man, his name is John and he is 24 years old. Currently away at college, he frequently enjoys visiting his home town to see his family and friends.
Occupation: Part time store employee
Education: Is currently pursuing a aMasters level education is Business Administration.
Goals and motivation for using the site: To keep track of his home town's events.

senerios

A local dad uses weatherahead.com to keep up with local events in the area and keep track of the weather. He finds that he uses it mainly for weather forecasts. Between gardening, outside sports events, and droughts, he finds it important to keep the weather forecast in mind while planning for anything.
A family out of town is visiting for a few weeks in a month. They want to know what the weather will be like while they are there. They also want to learn more about the town, and if there will be any fun events while they are there.
A tornado chaser is especially fond of this town because of the frequent big storms that occur nearby. He uses the weather ahead to keep track of the weather, as well as update his findings about the towns forecast.
A local restaurant uses weatherahead.com to find pictures of the town to use in their advertisements and menus. They also recommend the website to tourists looking to find more information about the town.
The high school soccer team uses weatherahead.com to check for weather conditions on game days, as well as advertise their events.
