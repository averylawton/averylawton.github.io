Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat

I chose the motserrat font because it is professional looking, as well as easy to read. It is a good match to the aesthetic I am going for, colorful and easy to understand.

Paragraph Font: Courier New

I chose the paragraph font to be courier new because it is easy to read and goes well with my header font.
