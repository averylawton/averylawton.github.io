Austin is known for its music, attractions, and most importantly, food. Support some of Austin’s best dining locations and RSVP a table today!

Black Star Co-op Pub and Brewery

Black Star is the world’s first cooperatively-owned and worker self-managed brewpub, sustained by a community of more than 3,000 member-owners. Black Star pays their workers a livable wage without accepting tips. They believe that tips should not motivate workers to provide excellent customer service.

Gourdough's Big. Fat. Donuts.

These are not your average pastries, and the donuts the size of your face being served from the trailer’s window prove it. People line up for these goodies, then sit around picnic tables to dig in, compare, and contrast. Locals and visitors alike come for the treats, and there is something for everyone.

Restaurant Jezebel

Restaurant Jezebel is an upcoming restaurant with a lot of character. The menu is ambitious, with combinations of flavors from all over the world. The prices are at the upper end of Austin dining, but so is the originality and service. It is a great staple of what Austin is all about, and the service can't be beat.

Upcoming Events

Self-Love Sunday Free Outdoor Yoga

Join Alyssa Clark on the lawn at My Vinyasa Practice for a FREE Community Outdoor Yoga! They gather at noon to flow under the Austin sun every Sunday. Alyssa Clark will lead you through a 60 minute all-levels flow focused on loving and giving gratitude to the most important person in your life – you!

Jaws On the Water

This summer, the Alamo Drafthouse is partnering with Action Pack Entertainment and High Beam Events to bring JAWS on the Water back to Volente Beach on Lake Travis! Get ready for scuba divers (and maybe other critters) lurking in the depths beneath your tube, ready to grab your dangling toes during on screen shark attacks!

Floral Buttercream Workshop

Join Sweet Bouquets Cupcakes for a fun night learning about buttercream and how to transform it into beautiful flowers. They will teach you how to pipe 5 different flowers and show you how to arrange them into a 7 cupcake bouquet for you to take home. All supplies are provided, just bring yourself, a friend and a willingness to learn!
