Join the Chamber

From the Austin Chamber home page we learn that "By joining the Chamber, you are supporting our mission to "provide leadership that facilitates the creation of a prosperous regional economy and effective advocacy for our members."" Chamber membership also supports numerous programs and benefits, including: Business advocacy, Programs & events, Connection on issues, Marketing & visibility, Exclusive perks, and Online tools.

Austin has three membership levels great for local business. The first level is Engage. It is ideal for retail, startups, and business with 10 or fewer employees. With this level you can,
- Get involved and stay informed
- Support the work of growing a prosperous regional economy
- Receive all core benefits listed above

The second level is build. It is ideal for a growing business that has fewer than 25 employees. With this level you can,
- Increased visibility and resources
- Enhanced directory listings: 2 listings and 2 categories per listing

The third level is Enterprise. This level is good for established businesses that want to deepen their engagement with the Chamber. With this level you can,
- Maximize your engagement and company recognition
- Flexible account planning, customized to fit your needs
- Broad range of enhanced benefits
